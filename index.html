About Me

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), under the supervision of Prof. Junwei Liang. My research interests include Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, specifically focusing on pedestrian motion and social navigation.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I earned the B.Sc. degree in Applied Statistics from Central University of Finance and Economics in 2020, supervised by Prof. Su Zhi, and the M.Sc. in Computational Statistics and Machine Learning from University College London in 2021, supervised by Prof. Ingemar J. Cox. Subsequently, I worked as a researcher at Tencent, developing deep learning solutions for virtual human.
